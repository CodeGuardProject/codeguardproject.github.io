KU Leuven — DistriNet, iDeas Research Group

The iDeas research group, led by Prof. Dr. Bert Lagaisse, specializes in designing intelligent, secure, and distributed software solutions. The group combines fundamental research with practice-oriented applications and has a strong focus on supporting software developers in building secure, reliable, and efficient software, both manually and with the help of AI code assistants.

Industry

Advisory Board

The project is guided by an advisory board of industry partners who provide practical insights, real-world use cases, and validation of research outcomes. The board is composed primarily of SMEs involved in software development, including:

  • Software product developers — benefit from early detection of vulnerabilities before products ship
  • Security consultants — leverage state-of-the-art methodologies for client engagements
  • DevOps engineers — gain practical knowledge for building secure deployment pipelines
